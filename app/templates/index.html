Who We Are?

We focuses on transformational applications of directed energy for applications ranging from photonic power transmission to relativistic propulsion.

We come from a major university environment with more than 4 decades of experience in technology and its applications from terrestrial to space missions. Our vision is to use new technological advances to better humanity through the synthesis of ideas and our core values. Our goal is to make possible that which is currently only a dream for many but whose reality can be realized.
